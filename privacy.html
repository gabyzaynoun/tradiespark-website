Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have. We've written it in plain English so it's easy to understand.

TradieSpark (ABN 72 847 046 578) is committed to protecting the personal information of our clients and website visitors in accordance with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use our services:

Information you provide directly

  • Contact details: your name, email address, and phone number
  • Business details: your trade, business name, ABN, service areas, and other information needed to build your website
  • Content and files: logos, photos, text, and other materials you upload or send us (files are stored via Cloudinary)
  • Payment information: billing details processed securely through Stripe (we don't store your card details)

Information collected automatically

  • Website analytics: pages visited, time on site, device type, browser, and general location via Google Analytics (GA4)
  • Form submissions: data submitted through our contact and project intake forms, processed via Formspree

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Building your website: using the content and details you provide to design and develop your site
  • Communication: discussing your project, answering questions, and providing updates
  • Invoicing and payments: sending invoices and processing payments for our services
  • Improving our services: understanding how visitors use our website so we can make it better
  • Portfolio: showcasing completed websites (as outlined in our Terms of Service)

We will never sell your personal information to third parties or use it for purposes unrelated to our services.

3. Third-Party Services

We use a small number of trusted third-party services to deliver our work. Each has their own privacy policy:

Service Purpose Data shared
Formspree Form handling Name, email, phone, message content
Cloudinary File storage Uploaded images and files
Google Analytics Website analytics Anonymised usage data, cookies
Stripe Payment processing Billing and payment details

We only share the minimum information necessary for each service to function. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these providers if you'd like more detail.

4. Cookies

Our website uses cookies — small text files stored on your device — primarily for analytics purposes.

Google Analytics (GA4) cookies

  • _ga: Used to distinguish unique visitors. Expires after 2 years.
  • _ga_*: Used to maintain session state. Expires after 2 years.

These cookies collect anonymised data about how you use our website (pages visited, time spent, etc.). They don't identify you personally. Google Analytics uses this data in aggregate to help us understand traffic patterns and improve our site.

You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on, or by adjusting your browser's cookie settings.

5. Data Retention

We keep your information for as long as reasonably needed:

  • Project files (designs, content, uploads): retained for 12 months after your website is delivered, then deleted
  • Contact and business details: retained while we have an ongoing business relationship, or until you ask us to delete them
  • Invoices and payment records: retained for 5 years as required by Australian tax law
  • Analytics data: retained according to Google Analytics default settings (14 months)

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct any information that is inaccurate or out of date
  • Delete your personal information (subject to any legal obligations we have to retain it)
  • Withdraw consent for any processing based on your consent

To exercise any of these rights, simply email us at hello@tradiespark.com.au. We'll respond within 30 days.

7. Australian Privacy Act Compliance

We comply with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the 13 Australian Privacy Principles (APPs). This means we:

  • Only collect personal information that is reasonably necessary for our services
  • Collect information by lawful and fair means
  • Take reasonable steps to protect personal information from misuse, interference, loss, and unauthorised access
  • Allow you to access and correct your personal information
  • Will notify you and the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) if we experience an eligible data breach

If you believe we have breached the APPs, you can lodge a complaint with us at hello@tradiespark.com.au or with the OAIC.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are designed for trade businesses and are not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

9. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information, including:

  • Using HTTPS encryption on our website
  • Processing payments through Stripe's PCI-compliant infrastructure
  • Limiting access to personal information to those who need it
  • Using reputable, security-conscious third-party services

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Significant changes will be communicated via email where possible.
